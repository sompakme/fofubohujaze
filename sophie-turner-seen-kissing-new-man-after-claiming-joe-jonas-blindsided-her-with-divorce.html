Sophie Turner Seen Kissing New Man After Claiming Joe Jonas Blindsided Her With Divorce

Highlights

  • Sophie Turner was recently spotted making out with a new man, Peregrine Pearson, a British aristocrat set to inherit a $272 million fortune.
  • Both Sophie and Peregrine recently ended previous relationships around the same time, with Sophie's ex-husband Joe Jonas filing for divorce in September.
  • Sophie and Joe have reached a temporary custody agreement for their two daughters, with designated custody exchange schedules until January 7, 2024.

Sophie Turner may have said she was taken aback when Joe Jonas filed for divorce in September, but it hasn’t taken the actress long to bounce back, as she was recently seen getting close to a new man.

Photographers caught Sophie making out with Peregrine Pearson – a British aristocrat that’s set to inherit a $272 million fortune – on Saturday, October 28.

Sophie’s New Man Is Also Recently Single

The pair were first spotted locking lips outside of a train station in France, only to reunite later in the day for the Rugby World Cup, where Sophia unveiled the event’s trophy.

Sophie isn’t the only one who recently got out of a relationship. Peregrine, a property developer, had reportedly been dating Princess Maria-Olympia of Greece and Denmark until September, when they apparently parted ways.

This means the new couple would’ve found themselves single around the same time, as Joe and Sophie were first reported to have broken up in August, with the Jonas Brothers member filing for divorce the following month.

Shortly after Joe’s divorce filing, Sophie launched a lawsuit, claiming he was withholding their children’s passports. The Game of Thrones alum claimed they’d been planning to relocate to her native UK before Joe’s decision to split, which she says caught her completely off guard.

Joe Jonas issued a response to his estranged wife’s lawsuit, denying her claims.

“After multiple conversations with Sophie, Joe initiated divorce proceedings in Florida, as Florida is the appropriate jurisdiction for the case. Sophie was aware that Joe was going to file for divorce,” his representative said in a statement.

“The Florida Court has already entered an order that restricts both parents from relocating the children. Sophie was served with this order on September 6, 2023, more than two weeks ago," they continued.

“Joe and Sophie had a cordial meeting this past Sunday in New York, when Sophie came to New York to be with the kids,” the rep added. “They have been with her since that meeting. Joe’s impression of the meeting was that they had reached an understanding that they would work together towards an amicable co-parenting setup."

Sophie And Joe Agreed To A Temporary Custody Agreement Last Month

Eventually, however, the estranged couple attended mediation and reached a temporary custody agreement for their two daughters, Willa, 3, and Delphine, 14 months.

As per court documents obtained by Us Weekly, the parents agreed that their girls would be in their mother’s care until October 21, and she had the authorization to accompany them on travels within the United States as well as the United Kingdom during this time. On October 21, Turner agreed to hand over their daughters to Jonas, who would keep them until November 2, including over Halloween.

Sophie and Joe agreed to maintain the temporary custody exchange schedule until January 7, 2024, with Joe having custody of the girls for Thanksgiving, and Sophie having custody for Christmas.

For now, Joe Jonas hasn’t publicly responded to the photos of Sophie and Peregrine.
