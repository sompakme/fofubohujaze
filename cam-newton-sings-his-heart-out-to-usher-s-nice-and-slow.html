Cam Newton Sings His Heart Out To Usher's 'Nice And Slow'

Usher‘s sensual jam, “Nice & Slow,” continues to send listeners into a mean body-rolling trance or belt out the lyrics every time it hits your ears, and for NFL star quarterback Cameron Newton, that was mildly evident. Although he decided to reserve his dance moves for the field, Newton sang the arousing words to the Jermaine Dupri-produced hit during an interview with Power 98 FM’s Morning Maddhouse segment.

For the “Sing-A-Long with Ms. Jessica” portion, the Carolina Panther didn’t hold back his vocals for Mr. Raymond’s nostalgic melody. He even added a mini-freestyle to show that he has a few bars stored up top. Maybe those studio sessions with Big Sean paid off.

We think he chose the path of a pro-athlete for a reason, but at least we know karaoke night would be a time to remember.
