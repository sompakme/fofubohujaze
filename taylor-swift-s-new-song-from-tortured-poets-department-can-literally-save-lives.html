As anyone who follows the modern music business will already know, Taylor Swift has written many songs that fans connect to deeply. Thanks to all the songs with quotable lyrics that Swift has written, her biggest hits have allowed her to build an incredible fortune.

On top of the fact that Swift's music has allowed her to have an amazing life, her songs have also helped many people. As amazing as that is, one of Swift's songs from "Tortured Poets Department" can potentially save many lives.

This article will reveal why one of Taylor Swift's songs could save many lives. Then, the article will look at fans who say that Swift has a long history of releasing lifesaving music.

Taylor Swift's "The Tortured Poets Department" Is The Perfect Tempo For CPR

Typically, when Taylor Swift releases a new album, fans and observers walk away with the same questions. How many awards will Swift win? How much money will Swift's album make? Which song will mean the most to Swift's fans? Who did Swift write each song about?

However, when Swift released her album "The Tortured Poets Department" in 2024, some people had a very different question. How many lives will Swift help to save? The reason observers had that question is The American Heart Association posted on Instagram about how "The Tortured Poets Department" can save lives.

"Taylor Swift's The Tortured Poets Department has 110 beats per minute - the right tempo for Hands-Only CPR."

That quote from The AHA's Instagram account was posted as an image. In the caption, The American Heart Association provided more information about how Swift's fans should handle things if someone needs CPR.
