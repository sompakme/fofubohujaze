Machine Gun Kelly’s Music Career

Machine Gun Kelly's musical career is a whirlwind of raw energy, genre-bending evolution, and chart success.

It all began in his teens when he honed his rapid-fire lyrical delivery and earned his stage name.

After adopting the stage name Machine Gun Kelly, he began performing at local venues, gradually building a following with his fast-paced vocal delivery and dynamic stage presence.

Early mixtapes like 100 Words and Running built a local buzz, but they did not see any form of commercial success.

Despite financial struggles and personal challenges, MGK's perseverance paid off when he released the breakout single "Alice In Wonderland," launching him into the spotlight and setting the stage for his debut studio album Lace Up in 2012.

Lace Up debuted at number four on the Billboard 200. It was certified gold, denoting sales of over 500,000 copies, setting the stage for future success.

Kelly's collaboration with Camila Cabello, "Bad Things," hit number four on the Hot 100 and earned 11.4 million streams, highlighting his crossover appeal.

MGK's transition from rap to pop-punk proved hugely successful, with Tickets To My Downfall dominating the charts. Several tracks, notably "My Ex's Best Friend," claimed multiple spots on Billboard's Hot Alternative Songs chart.

Machine Gun Kelly's discography consists of six studio albums, three extended plays (EPs), eight mixtapes, 32 singles and 75 music videos. Machine Gun Kelly has achieved impressive album sales, with over 2,866,754 albums sold worldwide.

This includes 2,500,000 copies sold in the United States and 220,000 in the United Kingdom. Notably, his best-selling album, Tickets to My Downfall, has surpassed 1,205,000 copies sold.

The Ohio-bred artist has sold 16.5 million certified units of digital singles.

Kelly's Acting Career

Although his music career is the major source of his income, Kelly's acting chops have landed him roles in big-budget films alongside major stars.

Kelly made his film debut in the 2014 romantic drama Beyond The Lights, where he played a rapper named Kid Culprit. The film grossed $14.6 million against a $7 million budget.

Beyond The Lights did not set the box office on fire, but it opened doors for Kelly, leading to roles in diverse projects like the Cleveland-set drama The Land and the techno-thriller Nerve.

His portrayal of drummer Tommy Lee in the Mötley Crüe biopic The Dirt marked a turning point, garnering him wider recognition and critical acclaim.

The film, released on Netflix, enjoyed global reach and contributed to his growing fame. He played Felix in the 2018 Netflix post-apocalyptic horror thriller film Bird Box with Sandra Bullock.

This success continued with roles in high-profile projects like Project Power, alongside Jamie Foxx.

In 2021, he co-starred in the crime thriller Midnight In The Switchgrass, alongside Bruce Willis and Megan Fox.

That same year, he starred in the action western film The Last Son with Sam Worthington.

Kelly also starred in the self-directed film Good Mourning, which debuted in 2022. Reports indicate that MGK has earned approximately $4 million from his film and television appearances.

Kelly's Business Ventures

Machine Gun Kelly's entrepreneurial ventures have significantly boosted his $25 million net worth, showcasing his business acumen beyond the realm of music and acting.

Like a true rockstar, he eschewed the usual celebrity skincare route and launched a unique venture: UN/DN, a cruelty-free nail polish line that offers 20 vibrant shades, reflecting his unique approach to beauty entrepreneurship.

Additionally, Kelly expanded into the food and beverage industry by opening The 27 Club, a coffee shop and restaurant in his hometown of Cleveland in 2020.

Despite initial controversy surrounding the store's name, which pays homage to celebrities who died at 27, The 27 Club has become a popular destination, serving everything from coffee to cocktails and food.

These successful business endeavors have undoubtedly contributed substantial profits to Machine Gun Kelly's net worth.
