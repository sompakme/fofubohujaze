Billy Joel has lived quite an interesting life. Besides being rich and famous beyond most people's dreams, he also has lived throught some interesting family drama. In the 73 years that he's been alive, Billy's had 4 wives, as well as 3 children. He's also dealt with a notable scamming issue and also lost money due to his numerous costly divorces. Billy's far from the only legendary musician who's had issues like this, though. Elton John's had his fair share of financial problems as well. Tabloids are often filled with drama involved in big-time celebrities' lives.

The "Who Didn't Start The Fire" singer has plenty of songs about love, so naturally, many people have been interested in his love life. Arguably, his most famous wife was Elizabeth Ann Weber. His first wife, Elizabeth, has completely disappeared from the public eye since their divorce back in 1982. Before that, she was involved in a lot of drama surrounding Billy's life. Numerous books and articles have been written about her, since she's caused Billy a lot of trouble and grief.

Who Was Billy Joel's Wife, Elizabeth Weber?

Elizabeth has been a disappearing act her whole life. The first major occurrence was when she disappeared from Billy and their mutual friends when Billy first met her back in the 70s.

That was the decade when Billy first became famous. His breakout song happened to be his most famous, his hit "Piano Man". It was a semi-biographical song about himself. After that song, many full-length biographies would be written about Billy. Large parts would be about Billy and Elizabeth's relationship, and boy was it riveting.

Elizabeth had a crazy effect on Billy. In an article Billy wrote for Rolling Stone, Billy admitted to attempting to take his own life. He attributed most of that to his depression, but his situation at the time was not very great. This was around the time he was 21. He was borderline homeless, a struggling musician, and, worst of all - he was in love with his drummer's wife. This happened to be Elizabeth.

After the culmination of the love triangle, Elizabeth disappeared from everybody involved. This was when Billy actually attempted to take his own life, but thankfully, he survived the attempt. Elizabeth would come back to Billy, and they'd get married in 1973.

After that, Billy would become the successful musician everyone knows today. He continued to be obsessed with his wife Elizabeth, and their love continued to hurt him.

Not only was he obsessed with Elizabeth romantically, but he also allowed her to be his manager. It was actually Elizabeth's biological brother who was the one that got involved in that crazy lawsuit with Billy. Elizabeth being involved with Billy's financials was the root cause of all of that.

What Happened To Elizabeth Weber?

Billy and Elizabeth would eventually divorce in 1982. Since then, she's pretty much disappeared off the face of the planet. What has she been up to?

According to Tuko, Elizabeth hasn't been publicly seen since 1982. As mentioned previously, she's always lived a very private life. Even the people closest to her have lacked information about her, such as when she went missing from Billy.

The whole situation is pretty jarring. Not many people can completely disappear from fame. Elizabeth herself wasn't that famous, but she was married to one of the most famous men of that era. Surely she couldn't have completely hidden away. However, there aren't any public records, let alone public photos or anything of the like.

What Happened To Billy Joel's Other Wives?

Since Billy divorced Elizabeth in 1982, he's married three other women.

There are not many people that have had more than two wives. There definitely aren't many people that have had more than two like Billy, aside from celebrities like Elon Musk or William Shatner.

Just three years after Billy and Elizabeth officially fell apart, Billy married another woman. This happened to be his second wife Christie Brinkley, the mother of Billy's first child Alexa Ray Joel. Their marriage, like Billy's previous, would go to last about a decade.

After their divorce in 1994, Billy would get married for a third time in 2004 with chef Katie Lee. This time, Billy's marriage would last for 5 years, as they announced their initial separation in 2009.

Billy married Alexis Roderick in 2015, and it seems that everything is finally going right for the legendary singer.
