What is Eugenio Derbez famous for? How did Eugenio Derbez became famous?

Eugenio González Derbez is a Mexican actor and comedian.

He was born on September 2, 1961 to parents Silvia Derbez, an actress, comedian, and director by trade, and Eugenio González Salas, a publicist.

What is Eugenio Derbez famous for?

Eugenio has appeared in many films and television series but internationally, is best known for The Book of Life,The Angry Birds Movie 2 and The Secret Life of Pets.

How did Eugenio Derbez became famous?

Eugenio was born and raised in Mexico City and expressed interest in acting at an early age, landing his first role as extras in soap operas at age 12 and made his directing debut in 1997 with a Mexican soap opera named No Tengo Madre.

Two years after his directing debut, Eugenio started a comedy show Derbez en Cuando.

A year later, he appeared in Carita de ángel in 2000 and also starred that year in Cómplices al Rescate} (‘Accomplices to the Rescue’).

In 2003, he produced a television series named XHDRBZ.

Eugenio is one of the few Mexican actors to have achieved international acclaim, earning international award recognition as well as starring in commercial American films.
