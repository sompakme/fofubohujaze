Steven Udvar-Hazy

Birthname István Ferencz Udvar-Házy Birthplace Budapest, Hungary Education University of California, Los Angeles Profession CEO Net worth $3,700,000,000 Source of Wealth SkyWest Airlines, Inc.,Aircraft Leasing Nationality United States Country United States Marital Status Married (Christine Hazy) Children 4

American businessman Steven Udvar-Hazy has an estimated net worth of $3.7 billion as of May 2015 according to Forbes.

Currently, Udvar-Hazy serves as the chief executive officer of Air Lease Corp. In the past, he was the CEO and Chairman of the International Lease Finance Corporation (ILFC) which is considered as one of the world's two largest aircraft lessors. The other is GECAS.

István Ferencz Udvar-Házy was born in 1946 in Budapest, Hungary. In 1958, his family moved to the U.S. from Hungary. He then studied at the University of California, Los Angeles. He is currently married to Christine Hazy with whom he has four children.

Together with two partners, he founded International Lease Finance Corporation (ILFC) in 1973. Currently based in Beverly Hills California, Steven Udvar-Hazy serves as the present Director of SkyWest Airlines, Inc.
