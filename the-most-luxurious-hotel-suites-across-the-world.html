Holidays are typically designed to explore new locations, discover new cultures and occasionally relax with a glass of wine next to the pool. However, if you find yourself splurging on one of these lavish suites around the world, you’re sure to find yourself spending all your time indoors, enjoying the finest luxuries in life.

What do you expect from your stay when you book a suite at a 5 star hotel? A complimentary bowl of fresh fruits and chocolates on your pillow? A snug bathrobe with fuzzy slippers? Free Wi-Fi connection? When you stay at one of these 10 luxurious suites around the world, you can expect much much more! From 24-hour private butlers to rides in limos, and from a personal trainer to your very own helicopter service, there is no limit to the luxuries you can find here.

Scroll below to discover all the amenities and amazement offered by the 10 most luxurious suites across the world.

The Princess Grace Suite, Hotel de Paris | Monte-Carlo, Monaco

Named after the eminent actress and Princess of Monaco, Grace Kelly, this elite suite is full of luxury and wonder, much like Monte Carlo itself. The 9,795 square-foot living space houses a selection of Grace Kelly’s personal possessions and favorite souvenirs, including her preferred poetry and books.

Spread across two floors, the Princess Grace Suite offers two bedrooms, a private pool, a Jacuzzi, a fitted kitchen, large living spaces and a terrace that overlooks the beautiful Mediterranean Sea. You can book this royal suite for upwards of $33,770 a night.

Penthouse, Faena Hotel | Miami, USA

Unlike the elegant ivory and cream shades of other luxury hotel suites, this Miami penthouse takes a bold take on luxury with the use of vivid carpets, animal prints and vibrant bedding. The five bedrooms all feature their own balconies and spread across 2 floors of this multi-level suite. Floor-length windows across the penthouse sport picturesque views of Miami’s stunning beaches and blue waters.

The suite’s multiple dining and living areas are perfect for entertaining guests and feature custom-made furniture designed by Frank Pollaro. You can enjoy this Miami sanctuary for $39,500 a night.

Penthouse Suite, Grand Hyatt (Hôtel Martinez) | Cannes, France

One of the hottest spots in town during the Cannes film festival, this illustrious suite has housed some of the biggest names in Hollywood over the years. The 17,957 square-foot suite features a lavish 3000 square-foot terrace that exhibits scenic views from the hotel’s very own private beach, the bay and even the Boulevard de la Croisette.

The massive suite offers 4 bedrooms, two living rooms, a walk-in closet, a working space and 2 dining rooms for you to enjoy at a cost of $41,000 per night.

Penthouse Suite, Hotel Cala di Volpe | Sardinia, Italy

Live in the utmost elegance and Italian indulgence when you book this penthouse suite in Hotel Cala di Volpe, Sardinia. The décor and art of the suite reflect the local virtuosity and the Sardinian culture amalgamated with contemporary luxury.

The 2,700 square-foot multi-leveled suite offers three bedrooms and three baths, a hot tub, a rooftop terrace, and even a private solarium and pool all for $41,177 a night.

Hilltop Villa | Laucala Island, Fiji

This villa offers amenities that are miles ahead of your average presidential hotel suite. Located on its very own private island, the Hilltop villa is ideal for large families and groups looking for a luxurious tropical getaway. The estate offers two guesthouses surrounding a massive panoramic swimming pool, a cook, a chauffeur and even a nanny.

Owned by the founder of Red Bull, Dietrich Mateschitz, the property spans 3,500 acres and even has its own farm and a mango orchard. You can score this luxury getaway for $45,000 a night.

Maharajah Pavilion, Raj Palace | Jaipur, India

You can’t get closer to living like royalty than actually staying at a refurbished ancient palace that once housed the monarchs on India. This ultra extravagant palatial suite features four floors, high ceilings, crystal chandeliers and even a museum showcasing India’s ancient artifacts.

The 16,000 square-feet multi-leveled suite is connected through private elevators and has its own hot tub, terrace, library, a private bar and a spa for you to relax in. Priced at $45,000, this suite will definitely make you feel like royalty for the night.

Ty Warner Penthouse, Four Seasons Hotel | New York City, USA

Resting on the 52nd floor of the Four Seasons hotel in New York City, this penthouse suite serves as one of the greatest viewpoints in New York with its 360-degree views and four glass balconies.

The living room is lacquered in mother of pearl, the 700 square-foot library has cathedral ceilings, and the furnishings include Venetian velvet, Japanese silk and Calfskin leather. It literally doesn’t get any plusher than this. You can enjoy this 4,300 square-foot penthouse and a complimentary Rolls Royce ride for $50,000 a night.

The Royal Villa, Grand Resort Lagonissi | Athens, Greece

The Royal Villa in Athens is ideal for those who desire luxury without bounds. This remarkable villa offers incredible amenities such as both, indoor and outdoor heated swimming pools, a BBQ grill, a kitchenette, a private massage zone, a walk-in closet, a gym and even a private beach.

The two-bedroom suite spans across almost 4,500 square-feet and is surrounded by 7,000 square-feet of lush green gardens. This incredible villa can host up to 5 guests and sells for $50,000 a night.

Grand Penthouse, The Mark Hotel | New York City

Initially constructed to be an elite $60 million apartment, this penthouse was later rebranded as one of the most exclusive and luxurious hotel suites in the world. The suite’s 2,500 square-foot rooftop terrace oversees one of New York’s most treasured landmarks, the iconic Central Park.

The suite offers five bedrooms, an eat-in kitchen, special room service by renowned chef Jean-Georges Vongerichten, tailoring by Bergdorf Goodman, a foyer and even a skylight pavilion. This beautiful 10,000 square-feet penthouse is available for $75,000 a night.

Royal Penthouse Suite, Hotel President Wilson | Geneva, Switzerland

This exclusive suite has played host to some of the world’s most eminent dignitaries such as Bill Clinton and Bill Gates in the past. Spanning 18,000-square-foot of space, the Royal Penthouse Suite in Geneva is considered to be the most expensive hotel room in the world.

Ideal for celebrities and politicians alike, this suite offers amenities that priority privacy and safety such as bulletproof windows, armored doors, a private elevator, a helipad and even a panic room. You can book this 12-bedroom haven for a hefty $80,000 a night.
