The Truth About Love It Or List It Hosts Hilary Farr And David Visentins Relationship Behind The

Highlights

  • David Visentin and Hilary Farr have a playful and entertaining dynamic on Love It Or List It.
  • Despite occasional conflicts, Farr and Visentin's relationship has been in question.
  • Both hosts have expressed their love for their roles on Love It Or List It.

Even though there is staging involved in House Hunters, it's still an incredibly fun show to watch. HGTV's Love It Or List It also draws viewers in with its beautiful home renovations and soothing spaces. As is often the case with the reality TV genre, the hosts are reasons why Love It Or List It has been such a popular show for such a long time. Real estate broker David Visentin and designer Hilary Farr are hilarious together and make every episode entertaining.

While people question how real Love It Or List It is and an extra shared the truth about staging on the show, fans also want to know more about Hilary Farr and David Visentin. Here's whether the co-hosts are friends or not.

Do Love It Or List It Co-Stars David Visentin And Hilary Farr Get Along?

According to House Digest, David Visentin and Hilary Farr have a friendship. According to many sources, including Closer Weekly and Heavy.com, several fans of the HGTV series have thought that they might be husband and wife. People always talk about the way that they joke around onscreen, and they do have a playful way of talking to each other.

However, they are definitely just pals, and there they have never had a relationship. When fans think that there is something more going on between them, it's likely because they have gotten to know each other so well over the years. Farr and Visentin have spent countless hours together since they began co-hosting Love It Or List It in 2008.

In 2020, Hilary Farr was interviewed about the series by Heavy.com and shared how she feels about her co-star.

Farr told Heavy.com:

“Well, David is just such an easy target. I mean, pick anything and I’m going to tease him about it. One of the things is the Italian hand flap, when his hands and his arms are just flying everywhere, like he’s going to take off at any minute. That’s one of them for sure. The other one is that he’s quite the motor mouth. He really is.”

Even though the hosts have never dated, people still talk about them and wonder. Even earlier this year, a fan posted about the HGTV co-stars on Reddit and asked, "Does anyone else feel like they’re in love? It’s that or the editors are messing with us. You cannot convince me otherwise."

Several fans responded that they are friends and that's it. Redditor @squatter_ wrote, "I think there is a lot of mutual respect and admiration, and great chemistry. A loving friendship."

In an interview with Realtor.com, David Visentin talked about getting along with Hilary Farr. He shared that while they weren't super close at the very start of filming the series, that changed and their friendship "manifested over time."

Visentin said:

"We’re good friends, we hang out outside of the show. We go out for dinners, talk about the show, talk about life.

But we’re both fairly competitive. So even outside of the show, we’ve been known to banter and we have to be very careful when we’re in public because sometimes we’ll just start in on each other. What you see on the show is kind of our relationship."

Farr and Visentin weren't friends before they were hired on Love It Or List It. It doesn't sound like they knew each other at all. Visentin explained to Monsters and Critics that he auditioned when his sister, who had a job at a TV network, suggested that he try out for the series. He called the audition with Farr "fun" but said that she doesn't recall meeting him.

Visentin also shared that sometimes, they have had trouble getting along, but those moments aren't included in Love It Or List It episodes.

Do David Visentin And Hilary Farr Like Co-Hosting Love It Or List It?

While Hilary Farr hosted the HGTV series Tough Love with Hilary Farr, which aired for eight episodes in 2021, she seems committed to her Love It Or List It co-hosting gig. It seems like she enjoys working on the show, and the same thing seems to be true of David Visentin.

Whenever Farr and Visentin talk about Love It Or List It, they have nothing but praise for the experience.

In 2015, Farr answered a question from Baltimore Media Blog about what she likes best about the reality TV series. She said, "It’s a combination. I love the crew and I love my co-host, David. I’m doing what I love to do. I’ve been able to open the clients eyes, as well as the audiences."

Visentin seems to love what he does as well. His Instagram bio reads "I am the dashingly handsome host of the hit show Love It or List it!" which suggests that having this job makes him very happy.

According to People, in a 2022 episode, a renovation of Hilary Farr's house was included, which allowed viewers to get to know the co-host even more. It's cool that she was able to share this part of her life on the TV show that she has co-hosted for so many years.
