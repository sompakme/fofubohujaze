Jaimie Hilfiger

Birthname Jaimie Hilfiger Birthplace New York Education University of Central Florida Profession Model Net worth $5,000,000 Source of Wealth Modeling Nationality United States Marital Status Single Children None

Jaimie Hilfiger is an American heiress, socialite, model, and fashion icon with an estimated net worth of $5 million. Born in upstate New York to Robert Hilfiger, a masonry contractor, and JoAnn Hilfiger, a registered nurse, she is also the niece of the legendary fashion designer, Tommy Hilfiger.

Hilfiger is an only child. She attended John S. Burke Catholic High School in Goshen, New York. She then went on to study Radio/Television at the University of Central Florida and graduated with a BA in Broadcast Media and a minor in Cinema Studies.

Jaimie Hilfiger began her career in the entertainment industry immediately after graduation when she moved back to New York City. She then moved to Beverly Hills and is involved in fashion with a work on a line of loungewear, modeling, acting, and regularly attending high-profile red carpet events. One of her primary aspirations is to be a reality star just like Kim Kardashian.
