Main Eventing Crown Jewel

LA Knight had a tough start to 2023, being involved in a terrible Mountain Dew Pitch Black match and going on to get hardly featured. However, in the lead up to Money in the Bank he was getting more mic time and began being presented like a star. He may still not have done much to find some title success, but his character and microphone work has been stellar.

A feud with The Miz was far better than it had any right to be, and he went on to challenge Roman Reigns for the top prize in WWE, showing just how far he climbed in the year.

Main Eventing WrestleMania

Kevin Owens’ momentum may have dropped off a little as the months have gone on, but he is still one of the better wrestlers of the year. Things kicked off in the Undisputed WWE Universal Title scene, main eventing the Royal Rumble with Roman Reigns and involving himself in the brilliant Bloodline storyline.

He main evented night one of WrestleMania 39, and whilst his Undisputed Tag Title reign with Sami Zayn was a little underwhelming, matches against Roman Reigns & Solo Sikoa and The Judgment Day were absolutely fantastic.

WWE Women's Championship

Asuka returned to WWE at the Royal Rumble, bringing a new gimmick and new momentum. She won at Elimination Chamber in a dominant fashion, and has been competing for titles on PPVs ever since. Her feud with Bianca Belair was good, and their matches at WrestleMania, Night of Champions, and triple threat including Charlotte Flair at SummerSlam all impressed in their own way.

Asuka’s reign as world champion was solid, and despite her being in WWE for several years, she has remained over and fresh, especially with a new presentation.

12 Dominik Mysterio Was One Of WWE's Best Heels In 2023

NXT North American Championship

Dominik Mysterio gets some criticism for his in-ring work, and to be fair he is a great way off many of the polished and experienced names on the roster. However, he has more than made up for it with his character work this year.

He is a heat magnet, getting the largest boos out of perhaps anyone on the entire roster. His feud with his father Rey Mysterio was brilliant, and their WrestleMania match was a blast. Things have been a little slow after that, but he is still one of the best chicken poo heels on the roster. Mysterio's heat has gotten bigger ever since his NXT North American Title reign too.

NXT Women's Championship

Becky Lynch’s involvement in the women’s division this year has been hugely important, with her helping to keep the division afloat in non-title feuds - something which has been a rarity in recent years.

Feuds with Bayley and Trish Stratus have been pivotal to keeping the undercard of the women’s division relevant, and she has (especially in the Stratus feud) found her edge once again as a babyface. A win of the NXT Women's World Title had gotten gold back around her waist too before she lost it to Lyra Valkyria.

Defeating Cody Rhodes

Despite being considered a part-timer, Brock Lesnar has certainly put in a shift compared to his usual schedule in 2023. This year, Lesnar has competed at six PPVs which is more than previous years, putting in some great performances too.

His match against Bobby Lashley was short but fun, he helped to make Omos look great at WrestleMania, and he has put his all into a feud with Cody Rhodes when he may have otherwise put in less effort to some of his storylines and matches in the past. Despite an ever-rising age, Lesnar looks in tip-top condition.

Rhea Ripley has been the star of The Judgment Day faction since it began, and her dominance in 2023 has been a great sight to behold. She started things off by winning the Royal Rumble from the number one position before going on to defeat Charlotte Flair in a match of the year contender at WrestleMania 39.

Her reign as Women's World Champion has been incredibly dominant thus far, with decisive wins over the RAW women's division, all the while putting in a great shift from a character perspective.

7 Sami Zayn's Year May Have Dipped, But It Was Still Great

Sami Zayn Was Incredibly Popular In 2023

Close

Best Accomplishment

Vs. The Usos (WrestleMania 39)

Main Eventing WrestleMania

There have not been too many better than Sami Zayn in 2023, even with an underwhelming latter half of the year. He entered the year as the most over star on the roster, and him turning on Roman Reigns kicked the year off with an all-time great moment.

He has main-evented Elimination Chamber (in his hometown of Montreal, Canada), WrestleMania and Night of Champions in big-time title matches, and even became one of the few to score a victory over Roman Reigns (albeit in a tag team match). The latter half of the year wasn't as impressive, but he was so good early on that he is still one of the best of 2023.

6 The Bloodline Carried WWE Through The First Half Of 2023

The Bloodline Storyline Was Tremendous For Most Of The Year

Close
  • Roman Reigns has continued to break records as world champion
  • Jey Uso has broken out as a top singles star
  • Solo Sikoa destroyed John Cena

It is only fair to include all members of The Bloodline in one entry, as without one another they wouldn’t have had such a huge year. The Usos and Roman Reigns both main-evented individual nights of WrestleMania, and they also broke records with title reigns too.

Their storyline has continued to be a major highlight on WWE programming, with it still feeling like a cut above any other story in professional wrestling today. All members have had a chance to shine in 2023. Despite Reigns having a part-time schedule, he oozes class and main-event stardom when he is on screen. The storyline might've petered out a bit, but it doesn't change the fact that 2023 has still be great for the faction.

Vs. Bianca Belair (Backlash 2023)

WWE Women's Championship

Damage CTRL were receiving a fair amount of criticism from fans due to their constant losses, but over 2023 the members have been getting better presentation, and Iyo Sky in particular has seen a surge in momentum. Sky had a big WrestleMania match involving Trish Stratus, Lita, & Becky Lynch, had a great title battle against Bianca Belair at Backlash, and won the Money in the Bank briefcase in a wonderful moment.

Her increased popularity and show-stealing performances all led to her cashing in the briefcase and winning the WWE Women’s Championship at SummerSlam 2023, later becoming the de facto Damage CTRL leader.

4 Damian Priest Won A Lot In 2023

Senor Money In The Bank And A Tag Team Champion

Vs. Bad Bunny (Backlash 2023)

Money In The Bank

The Judgment Day overall has had a great year, and as 2023 has progressed, Damian Priest has just been getting better and better. He would score the biggest victory of his career as he won the Money in the Bank ladder match, and would also become one half of the Undisputed WWE Tag Team Champions alongside Finn Balor. Perhaps even more than Money In The Bank however, Priest impressed the world with his main event at WWE Backlash against Bad Bunny which benefitted from an absolutely electric Puerto Rican crowd.

Priest looks primed for a world title reign at some point, and it will be deserved after having such a strong 2023.

He would even recieve a big rub as Lesnar shook his hand. Rhodes has continued to be red hot through a feud with Judgment Day too.

1 Gunther Is WWE's MVP Of 2023

A Record-Breaking IC Title Reign And World-Class Matches Put Gunther On Top

Close

Televised 2023 WWE Win/Loss Record

Best Match Of 2023

Best Accomplishment

Longest Reigning IC Champion Ever

After a great 2022, Gunther’s first half of 2023 has carried on in the same manner. He truly feels like an unbeatable champion who is playing his role to perfection.

His record-breaking Intercontinental Championship reign has seen yet another match of the year candidate, this time as WrestleMania 39 against both Sheamus and Drew McIntyre, and he has carried on from that with some strong momentum and star power, not to mention his record-breaking longest-time ever spent in a 30-person Royal Rumble performance too. Gunther's reign has continued to impress and he feels like a major star after becoming the longest-reigning IC Champion of all time.
