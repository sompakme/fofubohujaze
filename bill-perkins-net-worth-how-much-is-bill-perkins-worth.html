Early Life and Education

Bill Perkins, born on February 2, 1969, in Jersey City, New Jersey, had a childhood shaped by a family deeply involved in the world of sports. With his father and uncle both being football players, athleticism ran in his blood. Not only did he have a passion for sports, but Perkins also excelled academically.

Perkins attended St. Peter’s Preparatory School, where his determination and commitment to his studies laid the foundation for his future success. After completing high school, he pursued higher education at the University of Iowa, where he graduated with a degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. It was during his time at the university that Perkins developed a keen interest in entrepreneurship and finance, planting the seeds for his future endeavors.

Bill Perkins’ early life and education provided him with a unique combination of athletic discipline and academic excellence. These formative years shaped his drive, determination, and intellectual curiosity, laying the groundwork for his future accomplishments.

Business Ventures and Hedge Fund Career

After completing his education, Bill Perkins embarked on an impressive career in the energy industry, where he honed his skills as a natural gas trader under the mentorship of John D. Arnold.

Eager to chart his own path, Perkins founded his own hedge fund, Small Ventures USA, L.P, in 1997. This bold move allowed him to leverage his expertise and take calculated risks in the ever-changing financial landscape.

Seeking new opportunities and challenges, Perkins joined Centaurus Energy in 2002. As a member of the Centaurus team, he directed his focus towards venture capital and energy markets, gaining valuable insights and establishing himself as a shrewd investor and entrepreneur.

Presently, Bill Perkins serves as the manager of Skylar Capital, a prestigious energy hedge fund based in Houston. With a keen eye for market trends and a knack for identifying lucrative investment opportunities, Perkins continues to thrive in the world of finance and makes significant contributions to the success of Skylar Capital.
