Transformers The Cast Ranked From Richest To Poorest

In 2007, the first Transformers movie about a young Sam Witwicky, played by Shia LeBeouf, who tries to save the planet from an invasion by alien robots, was released in theaters. It was an instant hit with audiences, making $709,709,780 million at the box office, and birthed one of the most successful franchises in cinema history. Many of the Transformers cast members now have a net worth that can directly reflect the success of the franchise.
