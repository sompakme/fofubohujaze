Sophie has been playing guitar in MGK's band since June 2022, but the 27-year-old British guitar player had been working as an accomplished musician for years prior. Below we explain how her involvement in MGK's band came to fruition and just how much Sophie makes from being a part of it.

How Much Money Does Sophie Lloyd Make From Playing In Machine Gun Kelly's Band?

We don't know precisely how much Sophie makes from playing in Machine Gun Kelly's band, as the numbers aren't public. According to online reports, the Mainstream Sellout Tour made $33,680,513. Sophie is entitled to a small percentage of that number, so taking into consideration just how well the tour did with sales, chances are she made over the six-figure mark for her time on tour with the singer.

According to the guitarist's website, Sophie began playing guitar when she was only ten years old, saying that she used it as a way to escape reality. "Because I was a bit of an outcast, I had a lot of time to practice, and this gave me an escape from reality, helped me overcome my anxieties, and really gave me a sense of fulfillment," reads a statement by Sophie on her website. After becoming infatuated with playing, she began practicing on her Yamaha Pacific after school for at least five hours a night.

In 2011 she began posting her metal and rock guitar covers on YouTube. She's amassed over 800,000 followers on the channel, and her videos get millions of views. MGK actually found Sophie through her YouTube channel. She joined the singer's Mainstream Sellout tour after he contacted her via social media. "MGK literally just messaged me on Instagram one day, after seeing some of my videos. We Facetimed, we seemed to vibe really well, and before I knew it, I had my visa and was flying from London to LA for rehearsals," said Sophie during an interview with NME.

What Is Sophie Lloyd's Net Worth?

According to online sources, Sophie Lloyd has a net worth of $5 million. Along with her compensation for being a member of MGK's band, Sophie makes a lot of money from her YouTube Channel and the brand endorsements she's gotten from her notoriety on the social media platform.

Sophie is an incredibly talented musician and businesswoman, and before these insane rumors, that's what she was known for. Now her name is conflated with MGK and Megan's drama-filled relationship, something entirely outside the realm of her work. When her team commented on the nonsense news, they were sure to explain just how disrespectful the accusations were.

"Sophie Lloyd is a professional, accomplished musician who has been needlessly dragged into the media based off of meritless accusations made by social media. Any suggestion that she has ever acted in an unprofessional manner or stepped out of her relationship is untrue. It's disrespectful to her as a female artist and poor journalism and social commentary to be reporting anything else," said Sophie's PR team to Page Six.

Before her team released a statement, Sophie quickly reminded the public about who she is in a relationship with, her boyfriend of five years, Christopher "The Painter" Painter. Rather than hiding from the trolls on social media, she shared a sweet snap of the two on Valentine's Day.

Did Sophie Lloyd Really Hookup With Machine Gun Kelly?

According to Sophie Lloyd and Megan Fox, the infamous hookup between the guitarist and MGK never happened. Sophie's team quickly put out a scathing statement denying the accusations.

"Sophie Lloyd is a professional, accomplished musician who has been needlessly dragged into the media based off of meritless accusations made by social media. Any suggestion that she has ever acted in an unprofessional manner or stepped out of her relationship is untrue. It's disrespectful to her as a female artist and poor journalism and social commentary to be reporting anything else," said her camp to Page Six in response to the allegations.

A fan's conjecture stirred up all these rumors, and at first, Megan didn't do much to quell them. When a fan commented on her Instagram, "He probably got with Sophie," Megan didn't outright say no, but responded with a taunting, "Maybe I got with Sophie." Somehow, her response made fans think Sophie absolutely played a part in the pair's potential demise.

While Sophie's camp immediately put out a statement to cool off the harassment Sophie was receiving, Megan decided to deactivate her Instagram account altogether. It would be days before Megan responded to the rumor's validity.
