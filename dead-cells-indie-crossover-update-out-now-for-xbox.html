Dead Cells' indie crossover update out now for Xbox

Everyone is Here Vol. II, the latest indie crossover for Dead Cells featuring Terraria, Risk of Rain, Hotline Miami, and more, is available now for consoles.

Update: It seems Motion Twin really did mean "soon" — the Everybody is Here Vol. II update is live now for consoles, having been available for PC since last week. It features crossovers with Slay The Spire, Katana Zero, and more.

Original story: Another indie crossover for Dead Cells will feature Terraria, Risk of Rain, Hotline Miami, and more in Everyone is Here Vol. II — "coming soon to consoles."

The upcoming indie crossover for Dead Cells sees it join forces with Terraria, Risk of Rain, Shovel Knight, Katana Zero, and Slay The Spire. Check out the gameplay trailer below.

Dead Cells gets another indie crossover on consoles "soon"

The Everyone is Here Vol. II indie crossover adds skins, skills, and weapons from Hotline Miami, Terraria, Risk of Rain, Katana Zero, Shovel Knight, and Slay the Spire.

More specifically, Shovel Knight will add a new outfit and the Brutality weapon King Scepter, while Hotline Miami adds the modernized Bomber outfit and the Brutality/Survival weapon of the Baseball Bat. Katana Zero adds the new Zero outfit and the Tactics/Survival weapon of Throwable Objects, while Risk of Rain 2 adds the Commando outfit and the Brutality/Tactics weapon of Laser Glaive. Then there's the new familiar outfit and the Starfury Brutality/Tactics weapon from Terraria, and the new Ironclad outfit and the Diverse Deck as a new Colourless power. There will also be six new Lore rooms.

Everyone is Here Vol. II is available now for PC, and "coming soon" to consoles. What do you make of it? Let us know in the comments, and check out our list of everything coming to Game Pass in 2022 and beyond to see what else is on the horizon!
