Draya Michele, born Andraya Michele Howard on January 23, 1985, in Reading, Pennsylvania, is a multifaceted talent who has made a name for herself in the entertainment industry. Her journey to success began as a model, appearing in renowned magazines such as “King,” “Lowrider Girl,” “Black Men,” and “Urban Ink”. With her striking looks and magnetic charisma, she quickly caught the attention of industry insiders.

In 2011, Draya Michele’s career took a significant turn when she joined the cast of the VH1 reality TV show “Basketball Wives LA”. This opportunity provided her with a platform to showcase her personality and talent to a wider audience. As a cast member, she captivated viewers with her unfiltered presence and garnered both fame and popularity.

With her rising prominence, Draya Michele seized every opportunity to expand her reach in the entertainment industry. She ventured into acting, appearing in films such as “The Perfect Match”, “Til Death Do Us Part”, and “The Sound of Christmas”. Additionally, she showcased her acting prowess on television shows like “Real Husbands of Hollywood”, “Star”, “L.A.’s Finest”, and “Be Someone”. Her versatility as an actress has contributed to her overall success and solidified her position in the industry.
