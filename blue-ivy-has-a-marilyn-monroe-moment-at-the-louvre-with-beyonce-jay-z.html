Blue Ivy Has a Marilyn Monroe Moment at the Louvre With Beyonce, Jay Z

Ms. Blue Monroe! Now that the On The Run Tour has wrapped, the Knowles-Carter family is enjoying some quality time together. Beyonce and Jay Z have stayed in Paris and enjoyed some sightseeing last week, visiting the Louvre with daughter Blue Ivy on Oct. 7. 

Queen Bey posted pictures of the family’s outing on her personal website on Oct. 11. In several images, the 2-year-old tot stands on top of a vent in the famed art museum as her red polka-dotted dress blows up giving the outfit a bubble effect.

In addition to Blue’s Marilyn Moment, the toddler’s proud mom also posted pictures of the little one posing in front of some of the most iconic pieces, including the Mona Lisa.
