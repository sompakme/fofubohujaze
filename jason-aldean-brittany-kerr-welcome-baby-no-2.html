Jason Aldean, Brittany Kerr Welcome Baby No. 2

One happy family! Jason Aldean and his wife, Brittany Kerr, have welcomed their second child together — a baby girl named Navy Rome Williams.

Kerr, 30, gave birth on Monday, February 4. “We welcomed our daughter Navy Rome into the world today at 12:45pm✨ 7lbs 12oz of pure preciousness💕,” she captioned a photo of her newborn daughter via Instagram. “We love you so much baby girl!!”

The exciting news comes less than a month after the 41-year-old country crooner revealed that his wife of nearly four years was “about to pop any second” while speaking with reporters at a party in Nashville on January 8.

The couple — who wed in March 2015 — revealed they were adding to their brood in a sweet Instagram post in July 2018.

“Sup everybody…… guess who is going to be a BIG brother! #thisdude #aldeanpartyof6,” Aldean captioned a photo of their tot rocking a “Big Brother” onesie. The following month, the pair — alongside their son and Aldean’s two daughters from a previous marriage ― documented the joyful occasion of finding out the baby is a girl in an Instagram post.
