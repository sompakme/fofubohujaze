Grace Van Patten Might Be Earning Millions, But She Still Lives At Home

Highlights

  • Grace Van Patten prefers to live at home despite her success and earning six figures from her TV roles.
  • She has a close relationship with her family and is afraid of being away from them for too long.
  • Grace Van Patten's net worth is estimated to be around $5 million, and her success on "Tell Me Lies" suggests she may receive a raise in the future.

Although Grace Van Patten is successful and accomplished, she still prefers to live at home. The face of Hulu's hit show, Tell Me Lies, is no stranger to the camera since she was a child. Grace’s father, Tim Van Patten, is well-renowned for his work on major TV shows like Sopranos and Black Mirror. It’s safe to say that the actress was born with a silver spoon.

But what’s particularly surprising about Grace Van Patten is that she apparently still lives at home despite earning six-plus figures from her TV roles.

Grace Van Patten Is Close With Her Family

Grace Van Patten reportedly lives at home (on purpose)

Who doesn’t love home? Well, for the The Sopranos child actress, it’s more than that. While many people her age, earning the kind of money she does, would have already left home and bought a mega-mansion or apartment, Grace doesn’t seem to be in a rush to leave the nest.

The Tell Me Lies star revealed interesting details to Vogue during an interview about a mini-series she was working on, Nine Perfect Strangers. Although the show was set in California, actual production took place in Australia for about five months.

When speaking to Vogue, Grace Van Patten began by expressing her excitement about working away from home, although she thought it was equally scary. The New York-born and raised actress disclosed her fear of being away from her family that long. Not only does she live at home, but she seems to have a close relationship with her family.

Grace Van Patten also confirmed her place of residence when speaking to Insider. The actress played alongside Bobby Cannavale in Nine Perfect Strangers. In the interview, she joked that she couldn’t get rid of Bobby even after the show’s production ended. The two entertainers live a few blocks away from each other in New York’s Brooklyn and constantly run into each other.

As a child, the Boardwalk Empire star grew up in Tribeca, New York, and followed her parents when they moved into their Brooklyn apartment. Although she is at an age where she can get her place, Grace is still adamant about moving out of her parents' home. She is still figuring out where to settle as her career grows.

What Is Grace Van Patten Worth?

Grace Van Patten has a net worth of about $5 million

Grace Van Patten first graced TV screens on The Sopranos as a child actor, courtesy of her dad, who was a director on the show. Seven years later, the actress would focus on acting and eventually carve a niche for herself in the entertainment industry. She has since secured solid roles in shows like Tell Me Lies, where she is the main star.

Grace Van Patten’s net worth has increased with every role she managed to secure. She has an estimated net worth of $5 million, though various sources seem to disagree on the exact figure. Some sources suggest Van Patten is worth as little as $500,000, while others suggest the actress is worth up to $6 million.

The star’s appearance on Nine Perfect Strangers may have catapulted her net worth further. Besides the series being a commercial success, it had numerous stars such as Nicole Kidman, Michael Shannon, Mellisa McCarthy, Bobby Cannavale, and Regina Hall.

It is reported that Nicole Kidman took home around $1 million per episode during Hulu’s Nine Perfect Strangers. While Grace Van Patten’s rate card isn’t at par with Nicole’s, she sent a strong statement appearing on the same show as the legendary Eyes Wide Shut actress.

Although it’s just speculation, Grace Van Patten may have made a significant amount for her role as Zoe.

The actress has also played in two movies distributed by Netflix. She appeared in Tramps and The Meyerowitz Stories which significantly boosted her worth.

Grace's Biggest Paycheck Likely Comes From Tell Me Lies

Van Patten's success on Tell Me Lies hints she'll be getting a raise soon

According to E! News, Grace Van Patten confirmed that the show, where she assumes a lead role, will be renewed for a second season. Lead actors in a series often get paid most of the money compared to the supporting cast. But this is only true if the show becomes a commercial success.

So, how did Tell Me Lies fair? The show got an okay reception, with over 80% of the viewers giving it an approval rating. It was renewed for its second season, showing it fared pretty well. What does this mean for Grace Van Patten? The excited actress can’t wait to start filming the show where she takes a lead role. This means more money in her pocket and a higher net worth in the coming years.

The announcement of Tell Me Lies season 2 will likely place Grace’s new net worth a little over $5 million. Its success may open new and bigger opportunities for the star who has been patient and persistent about her acting journey.

Perhaps she will be lucky to be nominated for and even win prestigious awards. As Grace Van Patten prepares to reprise her lead role on Tell Me Lies fans can’t help but wonder how bright her star will shine.
