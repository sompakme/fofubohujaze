Highlights

  • Fans speculate about Taylor Swift's relationship with Jason Kelce due to limited interactions and rumors of a frosty reception from Kelce and his wife.
  • Reports suggest that jealousy may be a possible reason for any tension between Swift and the Kelces.
  • Despite rumors, there is no concrete evidence of a feud between Swift and the Kelces, and some claims may be exaggerated or misinterpreted.

In the modern music landscape, there are very few artists who can even come close to Taylor Swift in terms of popularity. Due to her success, fans love to talk about everything about Swift, including her most popular songs, how she makes money, and her relationship with her parents.

Of course, one of the other things fans talk about the most is the people that Swift has dated over the years. With that in mind, it makes sense that fans want to know the truth about Swift's relationship with Travis Kelce's family. Swift's connection with some of Travis' family is well documented, but her interactions with his famous brother Jason Kelce are few and far between. This, paired with Jason's January 22nd, 2024 limelight-stealing moment at the Chiefs/Bills game, had really caused fans to wonder if there's a rift between the two...

Why Fans Think Taylor Swift And Jason Kelce Don't Like Each Other

In January 2024, Life and Style published an article about Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's relationship. According to that piece, the couple had their first major fight after Travis Kelce was in a funk following a loss and Swift had trouble contending with that. Later in the piece, Life and Style's John Quinn wrote about Swift's relationship with Travis Kelce's brother Jason and his wife.

According to Life and Style's article, Swift was receiving a frosty reception from Jason Kelce and Kylie Kelce. Citing an insider, the article claimed that Jason Kelce and Kylie Kelce haven't been welcoming to Swift.

“Taylor hasn’t had the best reception from Jason and his wife.”

More than that, Life and Style's article goes on to state a possible reason why Jason Kelce and Kylie Kelce don't like Swift. According to the insider who spoke to the magazine, Swift's friends believe that Jason and Kylie Kelce are jealous.

Taylor Swift Very Likely Isn't Feuding With Jason Kelce And Kylie Kelce

From the outside looking in, there is no way to know for sure how Travis Kelce's family feels about Taylor Swift. After all, even if everyone in Travis Kelce's family talked constantly about how much they love Swift, they could be putting up a public front. On top of that, Jason Kelce could seem to like Swift one day and then call her out publicly the next for all anyone knows.

That said, based on the information that is publicly available as of late January 2024, it seems like talk about the drama is overstated. Of course, that isn't surprising in the least, as most gossip surrounding Swift becomes overly dramatic.

The first reason to question if the rumors about Swift and Jason Kelce are overstated is that some of them are based on an inside source.

In many cases, inside sources have a lot of value when publishers carefully investigate those sources and their claims. In fact, inside sources have often played a key role in keeping the public informed.

More importantly, there is a specific claim that has been made about Swift and Kylie Kelce that shouldn't be taken at face value. According to reports, Swift was deeply offended by comments Kylie Kelce made during an interview. That report is very difficult to believe if you look closer into the situation.

On November 19th, 2023, Kylie Kelce gave an interview to Spectrum News Kansas City about a sports program she was working on for girls. During that interview, Kylie Kelce was asked about her family being under the spotlight thanks to Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift's relationship. Kylie Kelce's response was clickbaited into a dig at Swift, but when read in full, it seems clear she was only talking about herself.

"It’s not particularly my cup of tea to be in the spotlight or in front of a camera. I was a communication major in college. I love to be behind it. That's my bread and butter."

Even if people still want to construe that response as a shot at Swift, Kylie Kelce later denied that is what she was doing. In a TikTok, Kylie Kelce called out People's aforementioned headline that made it seem like she was calling out Swift. Furthermore, Kylie Kelce later posted a video of her daughter set to Swift's song "Never Grow Up".

Jason Kelce Stole The Spotlight From Taylor Swift

On January 21st, 2024, Taylor Swift, Kylie Kelce, and Jason Kelce all attended a Chiefs game against the Bulls. As the aforementioned article from The Daily Mail suggested, members of the media felt like things could get very awkward at that game.

While there were a lot of predictions about what might happen when Swift was in the same area as the Kelces, nobody called what went down. For the first time in a very long time, Swift wound up being completely upstaged by Jason Kelce.

Since Swift has been attending Travis Kelce's games, the cameras seem to find her, so they can capture her reactions to everything. By the end of the night on January 21st, 2024, however, all eyes were on Jason Kelce.

The reason for that is by the end of the night, a shirtless Jason Kelce could be seen celebrating his brother's success. Most notably, an elated Jason Kelce jumped out of the VIP box as he celebrated after his brother scored a touchdown.

As for any belief that Jason Kelce has a grudge against Taylor Swift, the chances of that seemed very small after a video from the event emerged.

As a shirtless Jason Kelce made his way among the fans in attendance, he spotted a young girl holding a sign dedicated to Swift. Jason Kelce then took the girl in front of the VIP box where he held her up, so she could show her sign to Swift. Jason Kelce could be heard explaining to the girl, "We're going to show this to Taylor, alright?" To say that most fans were charmed by this moment is an understatement.
