Fashion mogul Ong, 63, is married to Malaysian businessman and property mogul Ong Beng Seng. The family has a stake in the Somerset-based Mulberry luxury goods brand, and Cheryl Cole is one of a number of celebrities with a taste for the company's oversized handbags. Ong divides her time between Singapore and London, although her daughter Melissa has taken up the post of non-executive director at Mulberry. The rise in the Mulberry share price has pushed the family stake up to £465m. She currently has a net worth of $1.93 billion.
