Tammy Sytch DUI Sentencing Update

PWInsider.com is reporting that WWE Hall of Famer Tammy “Sunny” Sytch was scheduled to be sentenced yesterday afternoon after pleading guilty to three charges of DUI and one charge of driving without a license in 2015. The court hearing was continued and as of now a new sentencing date has not been placed on the court docket.
