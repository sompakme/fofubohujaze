Who Is Buying Beverly Hills Star, Farrah Brittany?

If you watch The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills you would have seen Kyle Richards’ daughter Farrah Aldjufrie. Kyle's eldest daughter from her previous marriage has popped up throughout her mother's many years on the show.

In Netflix’s new reality show Buying Beverly Hills, the luxury real estate agent — who’s known professionally as Farrah Brittany — finally steps into the spotlight alongside her stepdad Mauricio Umansky and sister Alexia Umansky.

At the center of this new reality series is the family business, The Agency. In the show, the father and daughter trio navigates the “high stakes world of luxury real estate in Los Angeles” with their eclectic team of agents.

As 34-year-old Farrah wrote in an Instagram post, “Excited for you guys to get to know us a bit better, see this part of our lives, and most importantly what it takes to be a great real estate agent in this fast-paced industry.”

So who is Farrah Brittany?

8 What Is Farrah Brittany's Job?

After attending New York University for two years, Farrah Brittany, graduated Magna Cum Laude from The University of Southern California with a bachelor’s degree in psychology and a minor in business.

Farrah is one of The Agency’s founding members, initially working as the Director of Client Relations when the company first launched. In 2011, Farrah became an estate agent and, according to the company’s website, has “worked on a multitude of multi-million dollar sales, co-listing properties, managing client relations and handling a wide range of sales processes, from listing presentations to marketing and escrows.”

In the role, she represents both buyers and sellers internationally but primarily sells in Los Angeles neighborhoods such as West Hollywood, Bel Air, Malibu, Pacific Palisades, and, of course, Beverly Hills.

7 Who Is Farrah Brittany Engaged To?

In November 2021, Farrah announced her engagement to Beverly Hills Car Club owner Alex Manos.

He launched his car dealership in 2009 with his mother. They specialize in uncommon or unusual cars at all price points.“The cars we sell could need work, but they also could be perfect. We have a car for $5,000 and that car is a million dollars,” Manos, 40, told a newspaper, referring to a rare 1961 Mercedes-Benz 300 SL Roadster. “That’s what makes us unique and brought us so much attention.”

It was revealed on an episode of RHOBH that Alex proposed shortly before cousin Paris Hilton’s wedding to Carter Reum. Because she was a bridesmaid, she kept the engagement under wraps until after the ceremony.

6 Farrah Brittany Doesn't Live With Her Fiancé

Alex and Farrah still haven't married. "I have my own house," she said on the show. "And then I also have our house, which is the house that Alex and I live in together."

When she was asked in a recent Instagram Story Q&A if she sold her house to move in with Alex, Farrah responded, "Nope! I still have it."

She has admitted that she has found her wedding dress, although kept the other details under wraps!

5 Who is Farrah Brittany’s Biological Father?

Farrah Brittany’s father is Guraish Aldjufrie. He met Kyle at a Tina Turner concert, but admitted his English wasn't that good so friends had to translate.

He married a 19-year-old Kyle Richards while she was pregnant with Farrah in 1988. They separated two years later. Kyle married Mauricio in 1996 and has three daughters with him.

"He [Guraish] loved me. We have a beautiful daughter together. I loved him. And I was just too young to be married," Kyle revealed on an episode of RHOBH when she spoke about her first marriage.

Guraish hails from a family of realtors who have a well-established business in Indonesia.

4 Who Is Farrah Brittany's Famous Cousin?

Farrah Brittany is very family orientated and is regularly seen with her cousins, including Paris Hilton. She regularly posts images of herself with Hilton, daughter to her aunt Kathy.

Farrah previously told The Daily Dish, "Our immediate family is super, super tight. We're all supportive of each other. We all love being together, but also my cousins and I are extremely tight as well, like Kim [Richards'] kids [and] my other cousins. We travel together multiple times a year. We're together every weekend. Obviously, our family has their ups and downs, as everyone knows, but overall we have a very strong bond, so it's pretty awesome."

3 Farrah Brittany Is Close To Her Family

Farrah Brittany is close to her sister Alexia, who also works at The Agency. The pair share screen time on Buying Beverly Hills where Farrah takes her younger sister

Her sister, Sophia Kylie Umansky is a model as well as an influencer. The model walked on the runway at New York Fashion Week.

She is also close to Mauricio. "I certainly don't think of her as my stepdaughter," he says on the show.

"He's very, very close with my mom and Mauricio, like they're great, great, great friends," Farrah revealed to The Daily Dish. "I always say I have two dads; they both raised me. They both get along amazingly. They've done [real estate] deals together. My dad refers clients to Mauricio all the time. We all get together, he comes to our house for Christmas, Thanksgiving, it's all good."

2 Farrah Brittany Has Infamous Halloween Parties

Farrah's birthday falls on Halloween. Her family throw huge parties, called Farrahween, to celebrate her. Many of these parties appear on RHOBH and on the first series of Buying Beverly Hills.

Farrah’s 30th birthday party was heavily featured on the Bravo reality show in 2018. Farrah goes big on costumes, often wearing over-the-top and sexy looks.

1 How Much Is Farrah Brittany Worth?

Farrah Brittany’s net worth is reported to be close to $20 million. She has accumulated a huge fortune for herself by co-founding The Agency.

Farrah and her team of real estate agents are considered one of the highest-producing agents in REAL Trends annual list of The Wall Street Journal. According to reports, she sells 10 properties every year and her latest listing is around $10 million.
