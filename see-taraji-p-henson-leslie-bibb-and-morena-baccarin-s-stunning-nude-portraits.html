See Taraji P. Henson, Leslie Bibb and Morena Baccarin's Stunning Nude Portraits

Maria Menounos and Debra Messing aren’t the only actresses baring it all for Allure.

In a shoot for the magazine’s upcoming issue, some of television’s hottest leading ladies–including Person of Interest‘s Taraji P. Henson, GCB‘s Leslie Bibb, and V‘s Morena Baccarin–stun in gorgeous black-and-white portraits, shot by legendary photographer Patrick Demarchelier.

Like Menounos and Messing, 41-year-old Henson lounges seductively in bed, while Bibb, 37, flaunts her assets in front of a window. Draped in nothing but diamonds, Baccarin, 32, checks out her reflection in a mirror.

For behind-the-scenes video from Demarchelier’s sexy shoot–or to see a slideshow of the whole portfolio–check out Allure.com.
