What Is The Cast Of Felicity Up To Today?

Felicity was a critically acclaimed drama series created by J.J. Abrams about a girl who goes off to college and finds herself in a love triangle with two handsome young men. The series ran for four seasons from 1998 until 2002 and reportedly lost a lot of viewers after series star Keri Russell cut her hair. But that's a whole other story.

Related: Here's What Scott Speedman Has Been Up To Since Underworld

Fans may be wondering what the cast of Felicity has been up to in the two decades since the show went off the air. Most of them are still working in television, believe it or not, and most of them are married with children. From Scott Speedman to Scott Foley, let's find out who is doing what and where we can find the stars today.

8 Keri Russell Was On The Americans

Russell took a bit of a break from acting during the pandemic as she gave birth to a son named Sam in 2016 and had a young child to take care of. She was a series regular on the FX series The Americans with Matthew Rhys, who she is now married to, from 2013 until 2018. She also had a role in Star Wars: Episode IX - The Rise of Skywalker in 2019. Along with her son, Sam, she has two children from a previous marriage. She met Rhys while filming The Americans.

7 Scott Speedman Is On Grey's Anatomy

Scott Speedman is currently working on Grey's Anatomyin the role of Dr. Nick Marsh. He previously had a series regular role on the series Animal Kingdom as well as the Netflix series, You. Speedman has also had plenty of film projects in recent years including Run This Town, The Monster, Best Sellers, Sharp Stick, and Crimes of the Future. Speedman welcomed a daughter into the world in October 2021 with his girlfriend, Lindsay Rae Hoffman.

6 Scott Foley Has Been Starring In Various TV Shows

Scott Foley has recently starred in short-lived television shows such as The Big Leap and Whiskey Cavalier. He also starred on Scandal from 2013 until 2018. The actor has also appeared in series such as True Blood and Undateable. Foley has been married to actress Marika Dominczyk since 2007 and the two share three kids together. The actor was previously married to actress Jennifer Garner from 2000 until 2004. Foley has recently joined the cast of Girls On The Bus for HBO Max.

5 Tangi Miller Has Been Making Movies

Tangi Miller has mostly been making movies lately. Most recently, the actress has starred in 72 Hours, DILF Diaries, Boxing Day: A Day After Christmas, Leroy, and Entanglement. Miller also directed the film DILF Diaries in 2016. Not much is known about her personal life and appears to be single currently and doesn't have any children.

4 Greg Grunberg Is Still Acting

Greg Grunberg's is still acting to this day and his most recent projects include a couple of episodes of The Rookie, movies such as Satanic Hispanics, The Fabelmans, Burning Dog, and Star Wars: Episode IX - The Rise of Skywalker. Grunberg has been married to Elizabeth Dawn Wershow since 1992, long before Felicity, and the two share three children together. Grunberg has also spent his years working with the Epilepsy Foundation, as epilepsy is something his son, Jake, suffers from.

3 Amanda Foreman Hasn't Acted Lately

Amanda Foreman's last acting job was on the MTV series Awkward. as Mrs. McKibben, which was a recurring role. She appeared in eight episodes of the comedy series. Previous to her role on the comedy series, she guest-starred in episodes of Selfie and The Catch. According to her resume on IMDb, she hasn't acted since 2016.

2 Amy Jo Johnson Has Been Directing

Along with continuing to act, Amy Jo Johnson has been working as a director. She has directed projects such as an episode of Superman & Lois, Tammy's Always Dying, and The Space Between. When it comes to being in front of the camera, Johnson has had roles in projects such as Space & Time, Between Waves, The Space Between, and appeared in eight episodes of the television series, Covert Affairs. She also had a cameo in the 2017 Power Rangers movie. She shares one daughter with her ex-husband, Olivier Giner, whom she split from in 2017.

1 Ian Gomez Was On Cougar Town

Ian Gomez has had quite the career since Felicity ended. He had a series regular role on the comedy series Cougar Town alongside Courteney Cox and had a recurring role on The Morning Show with Jennifer Aniston. He has also had guest roles on series such as Fresh Off the Boat, Superstore, American Housewife, and Single Drunk Female. He also had a role in the comedy film Barb and Star Go to Vista Del Mar. Gomez was married to actress Nia Vardalos from 1993 until 2018 and the two share a daughter together that they adopted. In a joint statement to People, the two said that they had been separated "for a lengthy period of time. Our relationship became a friendship so the decision to end the marriage is completely mutual and amicable."
