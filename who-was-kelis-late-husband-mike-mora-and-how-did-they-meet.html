Who Was Kelis' Late Husband Mike Mora, And How Did They Meet?

Kelis Rogers, also known as Kelis, gained popularity in the music industry in the early 2000s through her successful tracks such as "Not in Love" and "Milkshake." The singer also worked with artists including Busta Rhymes, Disclosure, No Doubt, Calvin Harris, Enrique Iglesias, and Björk, to mention a few.

Her successful career also led to her receiving Brit awards, NME Awards, Q Awards, and two Grammy nominations. While she has been a household name in the music industry, her personal life is also never out of the limelight. Let’s take a look at who was her late husband Mike Mora and how they met.

Kelis Met Her Husband Mike Mora After Her Divorce

In 2005, rapper Kelis wed rapper Nas. For a while, the couple was idolized because both were hugely successful on their own. In 2009, Kelis and Nas made the joyful announcement of expecting their first child together, but their marriage was deteriorating simultaneously.

What initially seemed like a perfect match in the music industry ended in heartbreak, leading Kelis to file for divorce while she was seven months pregnant. It wasn't until 2018 that Kelis came forward and said the rapper had assaulted her.

Following a period of healing from her tumultuous relationship with Nas, Kelis found love once again, this time with Mike Mora. The couple tied the knot in 2014, embarking on a journey of love and creativity together.

The couple, who has two kids together, shared a vision of a beautiful future, purchasing a farm in 2018 with the intention of cultivating their own food and living a sustainable lifestyle.

It was a testament to their shared values and dreams. Along with Kelis, Mike relocated to a farm, where the singer said that the two of them manage every part of the property by themselves.

Speaking to an interview, she revealed, "It’s pretty much my husband and me looking after the farm. You get up in the morning and do what you gotta do, and then you look around and you’re like: Why does it still look like this? Oh my God!"

Unfortunately, their idyllic life took an unexpected turn when Mike's health began to deteriorate.

Mike Mora Was A Photographer

While Mike Mora is more well-known as Kelis’ late husband than as a photographer, it did not hinder him to continue his passion in that field.

He was deeply passionate about his craft, having mastered various genres such as fine art, portraits, and lifestyle photography. His incredible work can be seen on his Instagram account @mikemorafotos, which has over 18,000 followers today.

In addition to his online presence, Mike also had a personal website where he sold his personalized photos and services. He earned a good living as a photographer and real estate agent. However, things didn’t turn out the way he thought they would.

Mike Mora Passed Away From Cancer In 2022

In 2020, shortly after moving to a farm outside Los Angeles, Mike started experiencing excruciating stomach pain. At first, he attributed it to the demanding physical work on the farm. However, as the pain persisted, he sought medical attention.

In September 2020, Mike took to Instagram to share the shocking news that he had been diagnosed with stage 4 stomach cancer, also known as gastric adenocarcinoma.

In his heartfelt Instagram post, Mike expressed his initial belief that he was dealing with a simple ulcer. Little did he know that his life was about to take a devastating turn. As he lay in a hospital bed during the midst of the pandemic, he felt the weight of his diagnosis and the loneliness that accompanied it.

Mike shared his story not only to raise awareness about his condition but also to inspire others facing similar challenges.

Despite the grim prognosis, Mike remained determined to make the most of his time and spread a message of hope to others. In his Instagram post, he mentioned his three children and his wife, expressing his desire to help those grappling with life-altering diseases.

He wanted to show that it was possible to navigate through the darkest times and emerge stronger on the other side.

He acknowledged the uncertainty and doubt that often accompanies a serious illness but urged others not to take anything for granted. Apparently, his candidness and vulnerability struck a chord with many. His words served as a moving reminder to cherish every moment and to reach out to loved ones, regardless of the circumstances.
