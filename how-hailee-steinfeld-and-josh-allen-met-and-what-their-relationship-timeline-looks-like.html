How Hailee Steinfeld And Josh Allen Met, And What Their Relationship Timeline Looks Like

Highlights

  • Despite being linked since May 2023, Hailee Steinfeld and Josh Allen have kept their relationship very private.
  • The couple has been spotted together at various events, suggesting that things are getting serious.
  • Their relationship timeline is marked by secrecy, with Josh Allen expressing discomfort over media attention.

Unlike Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's once secret and now very public relationship, fans know next to nothing about Hailee Steinfeld's relationship with another NFL player. Yes, Swift isn't the only music superstar to be dating a football player. Steinfeld has been linked to Buffalo Bills quarterback Josh Allen for some time now. However, details of their relationship are scarce.

However, following their joint appearance at Allen's sister's gender reveal party for her baby, more and more fans have questions about their relationship timeline. This article will delve into what we know about how the uber-wealthy Hailee Steinfeld and Josh Allen met as well as what their relationship timeline looks like up until March 26th, 2024.

How Hailee Steinfeld And Josh Allen Met

Hailee Steinfeld and Josh Allen have reportedly gotten very serious and plan on settling down with one another, according to various sources. However, fans are still unsure how the pair met in the first. Unfortunately, next to nothing is known about how the Edge of Seventeen actor and the NFL quarterback actually met. Of all the men Steinfeld has reportedly dated, the least is known about Allen. But given his comments about privacy as well as what happened with his ex-girlfriend Brittany Williams, it makes sense that they'd play things close to the chest.

  • Hailee Steinfeld is a year younger than Josh Allen

According to People, Hailee Steinfeld and Josh Allen were first linked in May 2023. The pair were spotted getting mighty cozy in New York City. Following this, an insider spoke to Us Weekly and claimed that the pair had been together for about a month and that things were going well. But nothing has been said about how the two actually met. Whether they choose to share more remains to be seen.

But, back in June 2023, the source told Us Weekly that the pair appeared to be getting serious fairly fast.

“Josh is the first guy in a while she’s been really interested in.”

Of course, Hailee Steinfeld has also been pretty private about her love life, including her previous relationships with former One Direction singer Niall Horan and her Romeo + Juliet castmate Douglas Booth.

Prior to dating the Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse star, Josh Allen was linked to Brittany Williams, whom he had known since childhood. Following an eight-year relationship, Williams reportedly cheated on Allen. However, we cannot confirm this information. Regardless, this resulted in some major backlash against Williams from Allen's NFL fans.

Since being linked in May 2023, Hailee Steinfeld and Josh Allen have reportedly gotten quite serious. Not only has Steinfeld been seen at Allen's sister's gender reveal party for her baby, but she has also been spotted getting close to his mother.

The Truth About Hailee Steinfeld And Josh Allen's Relationship Timeline

Following Hailee Steinfeld and Josh Allen being spotted getting cozy in New York back in early May 2023, the couple were seen again eating at Sushi By Bou in Chelsea. They even took a photo with the restaurant's founder, Michael Sinesky. Nearly a month later, another source told People that the couple was "still dating". This seems to signal the beginning of Steinfeld and Allen's relationship timeline.

But the paparazzi managed to confirm this for themselves on July 7th, 2023, when they photographed Allen and Steinfeld making out in a pool in Mexico. Mere months after meeting, the couple escaped for a PDA-filled getaway.

A month later, Josh Allen appeared on the "Pardon My Take" podcast and claimed that he was surprised by the level of interest people have in his relationship with Steinfeld. The Buffalo Bills superstar claimed that the photos of him and Steinfeld in Mexico felt "gross" and like an invasion of privacy. This was the first time Allen addressed his relationship with Steinfeld in public.

The NFL star is having a hard time keeping up with the actress's high-profile lifestyle.

A source built on what Allen said on the podcast in an interview with Life & Style by saying the following:

“Hailee’s accustomed to getting photographed wherever she goes, but it really upsets Josh."

In October 2023, Hailee Steinfeld and Josh Allen's relationship seemingly evolved even more. She was spotted in East Aurora, New York with Allen's mother, Lavonne. A few days later, Steinfeld and Allen were at a hockey game in Buffalo together. And Steinfeld was spotted at a few Buffalo Bills games.

However, Steinfeld effectively evaded questions about her relationship with Josh Allen when she attended the 2024 Golden Globe Awards. A few days later, she was spotted driving around Laguna Niguel, California with Josh Allen after his Bills lost to the Kansas City Chiefs, effectively ending their football season.
