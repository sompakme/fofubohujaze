Michael Connelly Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Michael Connelly (born July 21, 1956) is an American author of mystery and crime fiction books, most notably those starring LAPD Detective Hieronymus “Harry” Bosch and criminal defence attorney Mickey Haller. Many accolades have been bestowed upon him for his works, which have been translated into 39 languages. From 2003 to 2004, Connelly served as President of the Mystery Writers of America.
